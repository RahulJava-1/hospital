DESCRIPTION OF HOSPITAL

Established by DR. RK SHUKLA in 2000, we are into medical services from last 23 years, Chandra Hospital has a robust presence across the healthcare ecosystem. From routine wellness & preventive health care to innovative life-saving treatments and diagnosti services.

Vision

"To be the leading healthcare institution, recognized for excellence in patient care, medical research, and medical education, and to be the hospital of choice for patients and healthcare professionals alike."

Mission

"Our mission is to deliver compassionate, comprehensive, and accessible healthcare services to our community and beyond. We strive to provide exceptional patient-centered care with the latest medical technologies and evidence-based practices. Through collaboration, innovation, and a commitment to continuous improvement, we aim to improve the health and well-being of all those we serve."
