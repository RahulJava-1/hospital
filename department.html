Our Departements

Neuro Surgery

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is dedicated to providing advanced neurosurgery services, aimed at addressing complex neurological conditions and disorders affecting the brain, spine, and nervous system. Our specialized neurosurgery team comprises skilled neurosurgeons and medical professionals who are committed to delivering precise and compassionate care, utilizing cutting-edge technology and techniques.

Laproscopic Surgery

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is dedicated to providing state-of-the-art laparoscopic surgery services, also known as minimally invasive surgery. Our specialized laparoscopic surgery team is committed to delivering precise and advanced surgical interventions that offer numerous benefits, including shorter recovery times, less postoperative pain, and minimal scarring.

Orthopadics Surgery

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is dedicated to providing comprehensive orthopedic services aimed at restoring musculoskeletal health and enhancing mobility. Our expert orthopedic surgeons and medical professionals are committed to delivering personalized care that addresses a wide range of orthopedic conditions, from joint pain and fractures to complex surgeries and rehabilitative support.

Gynae OBS

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate obstetrics and gynecology (Gynae Obs) services that cater to women's unique healthcare needs throughout their lives. Our dedicated team of obstetricians, gynecologists, and medical professionals is here to support women in maintaining optimal health, managing reproductive concerns, and experiencing safe pregnancies and childbirth.

Oncho Surgery

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is dedicated to providing advanced thoracic surgery services, including oncologic surgery, to address conditions affecting the lungs, esophagus, and other structures in the chest. Our specialized thoracic surgery team is equipped with the expertise and technology to diagnose, treat, and manage complex thoracic conditions, ensuring optimal outcomes for our patients.

E.N.T

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is committed to providing comprehensive Ear, Nose, and Throat (E.N.T) services that encompass the full spectrum of care for your auditory, nasal, and throat health. Our dedicated team of E.N.T specialists and skilled medical professionals are here to diagnose, treat, and manage a wide range of conditions, ensuring your well-being and quality of life.

Ophthalmic

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is proud to offer comprehensive ophthalmic services dedicated to maintaining and enhancing your vision. Our skilled team of ophthalmologists and eye care professionals is committed to providing state-of-the-art diagnostics, treatment, and surgical interventions in a caring and compassionate environment.

Medicine

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is dedicated to providing high-quality and compassionate medical care across a wide spectrum of specialties. Our medical department boasts a team of experienced physicians, specialists, and support staff who are committed to delivering personalized care that meets your unique health needs.

Dental

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is delighted to offer a comprehensive range of dental services aimed at maintaining and enhancing your oral health. Our skilled team of dental professionals is committed to delivering personalized care in a comfortable and welcoming environment, ensuring that your smile remains healthy, confident, and radiant.

Phychiatry

Chandra Hospital Lucknow is committed to providing holistic healthcare, and this commitment extends to our comprehensive psychiatry services. Our dedicated team of experienced psychiatrists and mental health professionals are here to support individuals facing mental health challenges, offering a safe and compassionate environment for assessment, treatment, and healing.

Plastic Surgery

Chandra Hospital Lucknow proudly introduces its advanced and comprehensive plastic surgery services, dedicated to enhancing both aesthetic beauty and functional well-being. Our skilled team of plastic surgeons combines artistry and medical expertise to deliver exceptional results, all while prioritizing patient safety, comfort, and satisfaction.

Uro Surgery

Chandra Hospital Lucknow stands as a beacon of excellence in the field of urological surgery, offering cutting-edge treatments and compassionate care. With a commitment to patient well-being and a team of highly skilled urologists, surgeons, and medical professionals, Chandra Hospital is your trusted partner in restoring urological health.
